My Journey

My passion for front‐end development began when I first introduced a class of middle schoolers to robotics. My students and I were all hooked when we started to direct our LEGO robot using basic JavaScript commands; applause for the lift of the arms, uncontrollable laughter at the sputtering wheels. It felt odd to watch that half-finished robot hold the attention of 13-year-olds for more than a minute, but even more odd to realize that I was hooked as well. The more I learned alongside my students, the more eager I became to learn more on my own.

So, after a couple years of independent study, I decided to take the plunge into this career change. With a strong passion to learn, I devoured every lesson and project from our team at The Iron Yard. I’m eager to continue solving challenging problems while also creating attractive, interactive user experiences on the web.
